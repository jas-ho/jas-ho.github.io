--- layout: page title: What is FVP? ---

FVP (Final Version Perfected) is a task selection algorithm to help you focus on what matters most, right now.

Why use FVP?

  • Balances urgency, importance, and your current motivation, relying on your own intuition
  • Reduces decision fatigue
  • Adapts to changing priorities effortlessly

How it works:

  1. List your tasks
  2. Mark the first task
  3. For each following task, ask: "Do I want to do this more than the last marked task?"
    • If yes, mark it
    • If no, leave it unmarked
  4. Do the bottom-most marked task
  5. Repeat the process, starting from the next unmarked task

FVP helps you move through your to-do list quickly by focusing on what you genuinely want to do next, considering both importance and your current state of mind.
